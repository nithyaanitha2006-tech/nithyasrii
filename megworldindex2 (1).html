        Most websites take 2–4 weeks depending on scope. E-commerce and custom platforms can take 4–8 weeks. We'll give you an exact timeline after our first call.
        Do you offer support after launch?+
        Yes — every package includes a support window, and we offer ongoing maintenance plans for hosting, updates, and feature additions.
        Can you handle both the app and its marketing?+
        Absolutely. That's the whole point of MEG WORLD — one team across development, design, and marketing so nothing gets lost in handoff.
        What's your payment structure?+
        Typically 40% upfront, 40% at midpoint, and 20% on delivery. Custom structures are available for larger enterprise engagements.
